About Us

Our research group

Our group was established at Penn State in 2019. We are an interdiciplinary research team with ties to anthropology, biological sciences, and microbiome research at penn state. We explore how and why microbial communities change and adapt through time. Our research team is founded on five main pillars of research that survey microbial communities in everything from Antarctic ice to a human tooth.
