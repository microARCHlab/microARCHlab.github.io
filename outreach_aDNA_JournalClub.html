Ancient DNA Journal Club

MicroARCH's biweekly ancient DNA Journal club is a free online event open to all researchers from the undergraduate to the faculty level. Established locally in Penn State in the Fall of 2019, our discussions have grown to include a wide range of domestic and international participants. Papers for upkeeping meetings are emailed out to participants and advertised on our twitter posts. For more information or to be added to the listserv, email us at asg5573@psu.edu

Note: aDNA Journal Club will resume in August, 2021. Stay tuned for updates!
