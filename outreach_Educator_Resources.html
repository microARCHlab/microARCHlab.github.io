Educator Resources

This page is intended to provide K-12 educators with the resources to instruct students in ancient DNA, microbiome, and archaeological topics. If you do not find what you are looking for on this page or have suggestions for new materials, please contact us at asg5573@psu.edu and we will be happy to work with you to generate new content.
